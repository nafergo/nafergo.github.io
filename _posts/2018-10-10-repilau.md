On Sunday, October 7, I visited and photographed two megalithic dolmens ([cambarinho](https://www.instagram.com/p/BooyVjcAU4X/?utm_source=ig_web_options_share_sheet) and [repilau](https://www.instagram.com/p/Booyff1A9wz/?utm_source=ig_web_options_share_sheet)). This is a 3d model of the [Dolmen of Repilau](https://www.megalithic.co.uk/article.php?sid=14574).

This was created for an exhibit and my presentation at a [conference about Education and Heritage, Tourism and Communities](https://coneducacionpatrimonial.wordpress.com/) in Viseu, 18-21 october, Grão Vasco Museum.
