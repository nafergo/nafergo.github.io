Animated GIFs created with frames from animation tests for a short movie inspired by a quote from Ivan Illich's 'Deschooling Society'

_“School is the advertising agency which makes you believe that you need the society as it is.”_

Sadly, the project never reached production and is dorment (dead?) since 2008.

It was rendered with the Blender Internal render engine.
