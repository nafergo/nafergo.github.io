I’m Nelson Gonçalves, a freelance 3D generalist and educator living in Portugal. My work spans a wide range of subjects and styles with a special focus on architecture visualization, digital heritage and stylized cartoons. Additionally, I have extensive experience in higher education and training.

I’m available for freelance jobs and commissions. Feel free to contact me for any business inquiries or just say hello. Drop me a line and a real person will read it :)

### Blog and porfolio
This blog is my space to share and store some experiences and news, away from the crowded and noisy social networks. It's a record of events, experiences, thoughts and personal projects. All texts and images are entirely of my own work and creation unless otherwise referenced or acknowledged. It is not affiliated with any company or any other person. 

The portfolio is a password protected part of my site with samples of my commercial work, the “works made for hire”. It is protected with a “password upon request” policy because some of it can't be publish publicly, either because I signed a NDA or because I believe that the client expects confidentiality.
